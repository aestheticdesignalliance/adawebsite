McCarter Highway

The largest residential development in Downtown Newark creates an urban presence with articulated volumes and rich coloration, while bringing a heightened level of amenities to an underserved market.
777 McCarter Highway is a striking contemporary mixed-use development, a 33-story residential tower with a three-story garage and ground-floor retail. Conveniently located only 100 feet from Newark Penn Station and a three-minute walk from Prudential Center, it catalyzes urban living in a transit-rich neighborhood and was developed with Newark-born NBA legend Shaquille O'Neal as a partner.
